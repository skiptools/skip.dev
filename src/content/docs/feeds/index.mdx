## How to subscribe

Paste either URL into any feed reader — NetNewsWire, Reeder, Inoreader, Feedly, Miniflux, or whatever else you use. Most readers will pick up the feed icon and title automatically.

The release feed publishes `<icon>` and `<logo>` pointing at the Skip favicon, so it appears with the right avatar in your reader.

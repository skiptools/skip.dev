## Where to manage your sponsorship

Where you manage your sponsorship depends on the tier you signed up under. If you are not sure which applies, the email receipts you received at sign-up indicate whether your sponsorship was processed by GitHub or Stripe.

<div class="sponsor-grid">

  <SponsorCard title="Individual Tier" icon="person" color="blue">
    ### GitHub Sponsors

    If you signed up for the **Individual** tier, your sponsorship is billed through GitHub Sponsors.

    From the GitHub Sponsors page you can:
    - Update your payment method
    - View past sponsorship payments
    - Change or cancel your sponsorship

    <div class="sponsor-cta">
      <a href="https://github.com/sponsors/skiptools" class="sponsor-button">
        Manage on GitHub Sponsors
      </a>
    </div>
  </SponsorCard>

  <SponsorCard title="Corporate Tiers" icon="business" color="amber">
    ### Stripe Billing Portal

    If you signed up for the **Supporter**, **Ambassador**, or **Partner** tier, your sponsorship is billed through Stripe.

    From the Stripe billing portal you can:
    - Update your payment method and billing details
    - Download past invoices
    - Change or cancel your sponsorship

    <div class="sponsor-cta">
      <a href="https://billing.stripe.com/p/login/dRm6oH1pz6HnbxEfy0fAc00" class="sponsor-button">
        Manage on Stripe
      </a>
    </div>
  </SponsorCard>

</div>

## Need help?

If you cannot locate your sponsorship, want to switch tiers, or have any other questions, contact us at [sponsor@skip.tools](mailto:sponsor@skip.tools) and we will be happy to help.
